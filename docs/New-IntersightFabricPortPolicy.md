### -DeviceModel
This field specifies the device model that this Port Policy is being configured for.\n* `UCS-FI-6454` - The standard 4th generation UCS Fabric Interconnect with 54 ports.\n* `UCS-FI-64108` - The expanded 4th generation UCS Fabric Interconnect with 108 ports.\n* `UCS-FI-6536` - The standard 5th generation UCS Fabric Interconnect with 36 ports.\n* `UCSX-S9108-100G` - Cisco UCS Fabric Interconnect 9108 100G with 8 ports.\n* `UCS-FI-6664` - The standard 6th generation UCS Fabric Interconnect with 64 ports.\n* `UCS-FI-6652` - The standard 6th generation UCS Fabric Interconnect.\n* `UCSXE-ECMC-10G` - Cisco UCS XE ECMC 10G with 2 ports.\n* `UCSXE-ECMC-G1` - Cisco UCS XE ECMC G1 with 2 ports.\n* `unknown` - Unknown device type, usage is TBD.
